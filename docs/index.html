about

In design I like research, communication with users, exploring their motivations and expectations. I care about addressing business goals, solution testing, and creating design systems and libraries.

I believe my strengths are strong communication skills that make me able to explain my vision through the process, detail orientation, empathy, and cooperation.

I am passionate about community-building processes within the fields I engage in. I explore artistic practices and the creative process, fostering communities around different aspects of creation. Believing deeply in solidarity, I have taken leadership in several solidarity-focused initiatives, facilitating them from both a design and community perspective for years. My experience in science and statistics enables me to think with a data-driven approach, conduct thorough research, and effectively interpret design studies.
