about

In my design practice, I focus on deep research, communication with users, exploring their motivations and expectations, addressing business goals, solution testing, and creating design systems and libraries.

I am keen on community-building processes related to the things I'm in.

I explore art practice and processes around creation, so I build communities around different aspects of creation.

I also believe in solidarity; therefore,  I took leadership in a few solidarity-focused initiatives and facilitated them from both the design and community sides for years.

Experience in science and statistics helps me work with data and conduct and interpret design research.

My strengths are strong communication skills that make me able to explain my vision through the process, detail orientation, empathy, and cooperation.
